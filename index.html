A1 Day 1–4 Month 1 · Lesson 1

Morning Routine

目标：听满 100 遍 · 前 40 遍不开口，41 遍起跟读
📖 Full Script
I usually wake up around seven. I don't use an alarm — I just wake up naturally. The first thing I do is drink a big glass of water. Then I brush my teeth and take a shower. After that, I make coffee and sit by the window for a few minutes. I like that quiet time in the morning. It helps me feel ready for the day.
我通常七点左右起床。我不用闹钟——我就是自然醒。我做的第一件事是喝一大杯水。然后我刷牙、洗澡。之后，我煮咖啡，在窗边坐几分钟。我很喜欢早晨那段安静的时光。它帮助我做好一天的准备。

📚 Key Vocabulary
usually
/ˈjuːʒuəli/ → 快读常省为 /ˈjuːʒli/
通常；一般
I usually have coffee in the morning.
naturally
/ˈnætʃərəli/ → 连读易读成 /ˈnætʃrəli/
自然地；顺其自然
She woke up naturally before the alarm.
alarm
/əˈlɑːrm/ → 注意重音在第二音节
闹钟；警报
I set two alarms just in case.
grab
/ɡræb/
抓取；顺手拿；快速吃/喝
Let me grab a coffee before we go.
feel ready
/fiːl ˈrɛdi/ → 连读 feel ready → feelready
感觉准备好了
A good breakfast makes me feel ready for the day.
🔗 Connected Speech
wake up → wakeup
/weɪk ʌp/ → /ˈweɪkʌp/
连读 Linking
辅音+元音连读：k 结尾接 up 的 ʌ，两个词听起来像一个词。类似：pick up, look up, give up
use an → yuzan
/juːz ən/ → /ˈjuːzən/
弱读 Reduction
冠词 an 弱读：非重读位置的 an 弱化为 /ən/，与前一个词连起来。类似：take a, have an, buy an
take a → takea
/teɪk ə/ → /ˈteɪkə/
弱读 Reduction
冠词 a 弱读：非重读的 a 弱化为 /ə/（schwa），与前词连读。类似：have a, grab a, make a
first thing → firs(t) thing
/fɜːrst θɪŋ/ → /fɜːrs θɪŋ/
吞音 Elision
爆破音省略：t 在辅音前常被吞掉，first 的 t 在 th 前几乎听不到。类似：best thing, last time, next day
feel ready → feelready
/fiːl ˈrɛdi/ → /fiːlˈrɛdi/
连读 Linking
辅音+元音连读：l 结尾接 ready 的 r（实际读 ˈrɛdi），两词无缝连接。类似：feel amazing, call everyone
